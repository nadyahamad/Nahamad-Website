Dancing Vegan Yogi

www.dancingveganyogi.ntweb.co

Dancing Vegan Yogi was the first Irish website That I developed. This dancer wanted to renew her website. I used the principles of material design on this website, using Html5, css3 and Bootstrap.

THE PROBLEM:

The client wanted to renew her existing website because it did not reflect the look and feel of she wanted to show.

MY ROLE:

My role was to create a new website that reflects the different services this client wanted to offer as a dancer, like classes and workshops, as well as, to show her experience in different performances.

PROCESS:

Firstly, I met with the client, looking to understand their requirements and needs for her website, like keep users update about her classes, workshops and performances, as well as, showing some of her work in images and videos.

In this phase, my approach was to make some sketches and create a mid-fidelity wireframe of the homepage, as a starting point to test it with the client. Then, and after the first impression, I proceeded to develop the rest of the website and delivered wireframes of the internal pages.

RESULTS:

As a result, I've developed and maintaining her website, in an own Content Management System (CMS).

Finca Villa Nora

www.quindiofincavillanora.com

Finca Villa Nora is a country house-hotel with more than 120 years of history and tradition in coffee culture in Colombia. This website was created using Html5, css3 and Bootstrap.

THE PROBLEM:

The client wanted to renew their website because they didn't like the one they had at that moment.

MY ROLE:

My role was to redesign, deliver and maintain the client website.

PROCESS:

When I started the process of the proposal I realised that the website had a booking system that we do not manage. I had to let the client know about this at the same time as offering a similar service that would cover their needs. So, I offered them our message panel, that is a very easy system where our clients can communicate with their customers through an email and also keep a record of their messages.

RESULTS:

They accepted our offer and nowadays they receive around 5 messages per day asking for their hotel, that they manage through our platform, and they also have recommended us to their colleagues.

Bogota Burger

www.bogotaburger.com.co

In this website you can see a very tasty menu, with styles and flavours that change the way of eating hamburgers in Colombia. Was created using Html5, css3 and Bootstrap.

THE PROBLEM:

The Client didn't have a website, so they wanted to have an online presence to enhance the experience with their customers.

MY ROLE:

My role was to design and maintain the client website.

PROCESS:

As the client was based in Colombia, I sent them a form with some questions to conduct a research and understand their requirements, such as the information they wanted to offer, their type of audience, branding and colours and related websites.

Then, I followed the process to sketch and wireframe the design proposal to finally developed the site using Html5, css3 and Bootstrap.

RESULTS:

The result was a website to enables users to find their menu and location in an effortless way.
